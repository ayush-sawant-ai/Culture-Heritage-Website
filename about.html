About Us

Preserving Our Heritage, Enriching Our Future

Who We Are

The Cultural Department of India is dedicated to preserving, promoting, and celebrating India's diverse cultural heritage. Through various programs and initiatives, we strive to maintain the richness and vibrancy of our traditions.

Our Mission

To protect and promote India's cultural heritage while fostering innovation and creativity in the arts.

Our Vision

To create a thriving cultural landscape that connects people to their roots and traditions.

Our Work

Heritage Conservation

Protecting and restoring historical monuments and cultural sites.

Arts & Crafts

Empowering artisans and promoting traditional crafts.

Music & Dance

Supporting and preserving India's classical and folk performing arts.

Language & Literature

Encouraging the study and preservation of Indian languages and literature.
